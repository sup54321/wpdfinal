Urban Development Projects

Urban operation sector is one of the core businesses developed by WPD and Chinese Owned Enterprises, helping the Group expand and leap from real estate development to urban city operations. It has vigorously boosted the urban construction development, smart City, Green sustainable city, the local industries, transportation and urban landscape of China and in the Development of the City of Zhuhai, Fushan and the New Zhong Shan City. WPD and Chinese Owned Enterprises urban operation sector has successfully initiated a pioneer development mode of "government guided, market-oriented enterprise operation", and created a diversified and replicable comprehensive development mode for urban operation. The Shizimen Central Business District took the lead in enterprise innovation, expanding its business to almost all the districts in Zhuhai, such as Hengqin, Xiangzhou, Jinwan, Doumen, Gaoxin, Gaolangang, Bonded area and Wanshan. In the future, WPD and Chinese Owned Enterprises urban operation will focus on comprehensively expand its business across the whole world, building itself as a leading comprehensive urban operator in China and Worldwide.
