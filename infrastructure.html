Infrastructure & Energy Development

WPD aims to strengthen telecommunications infrastructure, such as investing in laying submarine and ground cables, satellite broadcasting, to ensure connectivity and access to information, especially in remote and rural areas. Developing broadband and mobile networks, supporting the provision of digital technology, Fintech, Insurtech, Payment Gateways, Payment Solutions, Digital Infrastructure, and E-Government by collaborating with telecommunications companies and governments to improve network coverage and reliability. WPD investor Macau Natural Gas Co., Ltd. and partner China Petrochemical Company have established liquefied natural gas receiving terminals and storage facilities on Zhiwan Island in Zhuhai. On December 15, 2006, the franchise was granted for the import and transportation of natural gas as a public service in Macau.
